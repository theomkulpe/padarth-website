About Metals and Materials Association

The Metals and Materials Association (MMA) at IIT Bombay is dedicated to advancements in metallurgy and materials science. Founded to foster innovation and knowledge-sharing, MMA has become a leading organization within IIT Bombay, driving research and education in sustainable materials, metallurgy, and materials science.

Events

From seminars to interactive sessions with industry experts, our events are designed to foster learning and collaboration. Join us to explore topics like renewable energy, advanced materials, and sustainable manufacturing.

Workshops

Hands-on workshops led by industry experts will dive into topics like materials for energy storage, sustainability, and advanced manufacturing. Gain valuable skills and insights from professionals.
